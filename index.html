About

Mahendra Singh Dhoni is a legendary cricketer from India, renowned for his success as a captain and finisher. He is the only captain to have won all three major ICC trophies: T20 World Cup (2007), ODI World Cup (2011), and Champions Trophy (2013). Known for his helicopter shot and sharp game sense, Dhoni remains an icon of Indian cricket.
